About Just Real Health Facts

Evidence-based health information. No spin. Just the facts.

Just Real Health Facts is an independent health education platform. Some content may reference products available through affiliate partner programs. We earn commissions on qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.

Our mission

Health information online is often either oversimplified, exaggerated, or written to sell something. Our goal is narrower and, we think, more useful: take peer-reviewed research and established clinical guidelines and translate them into clear, plain-language explanations of everyday health questions — so you can understand what the science actually shows.

How we work

  • We cite our sources. Claims trace back to published research or clinical guidance, so you can check them yourself.
  • We show the whole picture. When evidence is mixed or uncertain, we say so rather than picking a side for a cleaner story.
  • We separate strong evidence from weak. A large randomized trial and a small preliminary study are not the same thing, and we don't treat them as such.
  • We disclose our affiliate relationships. See our Affiliate Disclosure for the full details.

Medical review

Just Real Health Facts was founded by a board-certified physician. All clinical content is reviewed by a board-certified physician before it is published. This review is part of our editorial process and is not a substitute for personalized medical care.

What this site is not

The content here is for general educational purposes only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it cannot account for your individual health situation. Always consult your own doctor or pharmacist before starting, stopping, or changing any supplement, medication, or treatment — particularly if you have a medical condition or take prescription drugs.
