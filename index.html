About this site

Just Real Health Facts is an independent health-education resource. We turn peer-reviewed research and clinical guidelines into clear, plain-language explanations of everyday health questions — without the hype, the fear, or the sales pitch.

Every article cites its sources. When the evidence is strong, we say so. When it is mixed or uncertain, we say that too. Our goal is simple: help you understand what the science actually shows so you can make informed decisions and talk them through with a qualified healthcare professional.
